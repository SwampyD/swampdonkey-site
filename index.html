What is DONK?

DONK is a proudly useless, meme-level Solana collectible token - a tribute to every friend, cousin, or stranger who's ever been lucky enough to have a face-to-face encounter with a swamp donkey in the wild! You may have even been a swamp donkey in the past, are currently wreaking havoc as one now, or may be one in the future. Everyone has a little DONK in them!

It is not an investment, not a DAO, and not part of some complex roadmap to the metaverse. It's just a 69.420 billion supply digital donkey - holding a beer - wandering the Solana swamps.
